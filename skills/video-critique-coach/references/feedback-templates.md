# Feedback Templates

Pre-structured formats for delivering critique. Choose based on creator's needs and experience level.

---

## Standard Feedback Format

Use for most critiques:

```
## What's Working

### [Strength 1]
[Specific observation with timestamp if relevant]
[Why this works]

### [Strength 2]
[Specific observation]
[Why this works]

### [Strength 3]
[Specific observation]
[Why this works]
```

## Quick Feedback Format

For rapid reviews or when creator needs speed:

```
**Strongest moment:** [Timestamp] — [Why]

**Biggest opportunity:** [Issue] — [Fix]

**One quick win:** [Specific small change]

**Overall:** [1-2 sentence summary]
```

## Timestamp-Heavy Format

For detailed moment-by-moment feedback:

```
## Timeline Review

**0:00-0:05** — [Observation]
**0:05-0:30** — [Observation about hook]
**0:30-1:00** — [Observation]
[Continue through video]

## Summary by Section

**Intro (0:00-X:XX):** [Assessment]
**Main Content (X:XX-X:XX):** [Assessment]
**Outro (X:XX-end):** [Assessment]

## Key Moments

**Best moment:** [Timestamp] — [Why]
**Weakest moment:** [Timestamp] — [Why]
**Most improved from previous:** [Timestamp] — [Why]
```

## Tone Guidelines

**Be direct, not harsh:**
- ❌ "The intro is bad"
- ✅ "The intro takes 45 seconds to get to the value—most viewers will leave by 15"

**Be specific, not vague:**
- ❌ "Work on your delivery"
- ✅ "At 3:24, you drop energy noticeably—try matching the energy of your 1:15 moment"

**Be constructive, not just critical:**
- ❌ "The lighting is unflattering"
- ✅ "The lighting creates shadows under your eyes—a simple fill light to your left would fix this"

**Acknowledge context:**
- ❌ "This should have better graphics"
- ✅ "Given your setup, the graphics are solid—when you're ready to level up, motion graphics would add polish"
