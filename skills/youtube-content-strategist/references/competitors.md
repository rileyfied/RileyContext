## Competitor Categories to Track

### AI Education (Beginner-Focused)
- Channels teaching AI to non-technical audiences
- Look for: What tools they cover, how they explain concepts, production quality

### AI News/Commentary
- Channels covering AI developments
- Look for: How they balance news with practical application, clickbait vs substance

### Productivity/Automation
- Channels focused on workflows and systems (not just AI)
- Look for: How they structure tutorials, what tools overlap with AI

### Role-Specific Content
- Channels targeting specific professions
- Look for: Gaps in coverage for frontline/operational roles

## Research Prompts

When checking competitors, search for:
- `[channel name] latest video`
- `[channel name] most popular 2026`
- `site:youtube.com [channel name] [topic you're considering]`
