### Problem 2: Thursday has only 2 Team Leaders

That means NO Team Leader from 6am-10am on Thursday.
**FIX:** Add an early TL shift or shift Jana earlier. Or add Ian as 6am TL.

### Problem 3: Too many unavailable-on-specific-days cluster on same days
Mon & Wed are the hardest to staff. Many people are unavailable:

- Wed: Similar cluster of unavailability

This means Mon/Wed night crew is thinnest.

### Problem 4: Night shift length mismatch
Template has 13 night shifts, but 5 of them are short (2.5-4 hours).
These are essentially minor/student shifts. If a minor calls out, you lose that slot entirely because no one else wants a 2.5hr shift.
**FIX:** Consider consolidating some short shifts into fewer, longer shifts for non-minors. Keep 3-4 minor-friendly short shifts but don't rely on 5.

### Problem 5: The 11am-3pm day shifts are awkward
Two 11am-3pm shifts (4 hours each) are hard to fill with adults who want real hours.
They exist for lunch rush coverage but the people who'd take them (minors, students) aren't available at 11am on weekdays.
**FIX:** These are better as 10am-4pm or 10:30am-4:30pm (6 hours). More attractive, still covers lunch.

### Problem 6: Flex shifts are underutilized
Only 3 flex shifts (11:15am-3:15pm, 11:30am-3:30pm, 11:30am-6:30pm).
The first two are essentially lunch-only (4 hours). The third is the only meaningful flex shift.
**FIX:** Make all flex shifts 5-7 hours. Stagger starts: 10:45am, 11:15am, 11:30am with ends at 4:30pm-6:30pm.
