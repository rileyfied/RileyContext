This follows the Christ hymn (5–11), so tone must feel grounded and practical.  
**Tone**  
Serious but hopeful. Steady. Not slow.  
**Style**  
Piano-driven melodic pop with clear rhythmic phrasing.  
  
Piano with light bass and subtle percussion. Male lead vocal. Moderate steady tempo. Clear instructional tone. No long intro. No instrumental breaks. Strong rhythmic phrasing on “work out your own salvation.” Slight lift on “for it is God who works in you.” Clear diction. Simple harmony only on final repetition. Keep under 2 minutes.
