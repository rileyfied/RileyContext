## 7. Psalm 62:5–7  
Tone: Anchored, grounded, firm. Style: Mid-tempo piano + light strings. Strong melodic hook on “my rock and my salvation.”  
## STYLE PROMPT  
Piano-driven arrangement with subtle strings. Male vocal. Moderate tempo. Clear, grounded delivery. Emphasize strong melodic phrasing on repeated phrases. No instrumental breaks.  
## LYRICS  
Psalm 62:5–7  
For God alone, O my soul, wait in silence, for my hope is from him. He only is my rock and my salvation, my fortress; I shall not be shaken.  
On God rests my salvation and my glory; my mighty rock, my refuge is God.
