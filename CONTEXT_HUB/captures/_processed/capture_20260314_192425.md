Chick-fil-A Scheduling Rules & Notes:

- Always 4 openers
- Second leader in at 8 AM on Saturdays
- Third leader in at 12 PM on Saturdays
- First Evening leader in at 1 PM during spring break and holidays
- Minors get 4 hour shifts during spring break and holidays
- Staff in earlier during spring break and holidays
- Busiest lunch days: Monday Thursday Friday, then Wednesday and a later lunch Saturday, then Tuesday
- Busiest dinner days: Friday and Saturday, then Wednesday and Thursday, then Monday and Tuesday
- Busiest breakfast: all weekdays 6 AM to 8 AM, Saturday 8 AM to 10:30 AM, then all weekdays 8 AM to 11 AM
- Maybe schedule a second hospitality during spring break weekdays from 12-2?
- At least 2 A-baggers on at all times
- People who call out or are late cannot be scheduled before 9am
- Minors under 16 cannot be scheduled before 7am or after 7pm
- Certain people can only work 1 position
- Certain people can only work 2 positions
- Certain people can only work 3-5 positions
- Certain people can only work 1 day part (morning/day, day/evening, or evening/night), and some can work all 3 day parts
