Idea for a Gemini Gem: YouTube growth engine.

Prompt:

Create an Al mini app called "YouTube Growth Engine". Build this app
as a multi-step workflow with separate nodes for each stage. Do NOT
combine steps into one generation node. Required workflow structure
Node 1: Topic Input
- Accept a YouTube niche or topic.
Node 2: Subtopic Decomposition
- Break the niche into 5-10 high-potential subtopics.
Node 3: Audience & Demand Analysis
- Identify audience intent and content demand signals.
From Node 3, branch into three parallel strategy nodes:
Node 4A: Viral Shorts Strategy
- Generate 5 Shorts video ideas with titles.
Node 4B: Long-Form Authority Strategy
- Generate 5 long-form video titles and outlines.
Node 4C: Faceless Automation Strategy
- Generate 5 automation-style video concepts.
Node 5: Strategy Comparison
- Compare the three strategies:
- Growth speed
- Effort level
- Monetization potential
- Risk level
Node 6: Final Recommendation
- Select the strongest strategy with reasoning.
Node 7: Render Growth Blueprint
- Render all results into a structured HTML report.
Each stage must be implemented as a separate workflow node.
Do not collapse steps.
