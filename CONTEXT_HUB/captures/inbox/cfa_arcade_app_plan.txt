CFA Merritt Island Arcade App — Project Kickoff Capture
Date: 2026-03-24

## Concept
Collection of arcade-style training games and pop-up quizzes for CFA Merritt Island team.
Standalone HTML app. Timer countdowns, arcade vibes, beautiful UI.

## Game Ideas (from Riley)
1. Tap-in-Order — prioritize tasks in correct order (clean in place fans, greet guest, stock, change paper towels, wash hands, etc.)
2. Core Values Order — put 7 core values in correct sequence
3. How Do You Make It — lemonade recipe, other menu items
4. How Do You Ring It In — POS register training
5. Car Logo Quiz — identify car brands from logos (assets ready: 45 PNGs in CFA/CAR LOGOS/)
6. Make Model Color — identify car make, model, and color
7. If/Then Scenarios — "what do you do if..." situational training
8. Excellence Game — "You're doing great! But are you Excellent?" — teaches nuances of Striving for Excellence

## Core Values (from Riley's memory, needs verification)
1. Bring a joyful attitude
2. Strive for excellence
3. Don't whine, complain, or gossip
4. Be gracious, humble, and teachable
5. Clean as you go
6. Move with urgency
7. Character counts
